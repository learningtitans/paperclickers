About Paperclickers

Paperclickers was created by researchers at UNICAMP as an affordable solution for real-time collection of students reactions and answers in classroom. The project was conducted by Prof. Dr. Eduardo Valle and Prof. Dr. Renato Lopes, and M.Sc. students Jomara Bindá and Eduardo de Oliveira.

Privacy Policy

We collect usage data from Paperclicker application for research and development purposes only, to improve the overall solution through better understanding how the application meets the usage scenarios.

Paperclickers do not directly collect and keep any user's personal information, such as email, phone number, address or location. The collected data does not include any image, questions text or detected answers.

Paperclickers does not directly make use of cookies.

Paperclickers uses Google Firebase Analytics in order to collect the usage data; you can check how Google Firebase Analytics service collects and processes data in the following location: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

You can disable the usage data collection properly through the "Usage data collection" option inside the application settings.
